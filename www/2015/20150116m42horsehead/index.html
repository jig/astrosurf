M42 and Horse Head @ 100 mm | 2015/january/16

Shot with a Canon EF 100mm f/2.8L Macro IS USM and a Canon EOS 1100D (unmodified). 45 120 s frames totalling 1h30 imaging time at f/2.8.

The Celestron LPR was attached in front of the Canon lens with an ad-hoc ring.

Darks, flats and bias

North is up

Processed with DeepSkyStacker and PixInsight (non-lineal: HDRMultiscaleTransform and Erosion).
