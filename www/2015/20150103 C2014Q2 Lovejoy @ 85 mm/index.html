Comet 2014/Q2 Lovejoy @ 85 mm | 2015/january/3

Shot with an 85 mm f/1.8 L stopped down to f/2.8, Canon EOS 1100D. 203 frames 2 seconds each totalling 6 m 43 s, 1600 ISO/ASA. No sidereal tracking (shot with a bare tripod). Click here to see an 1 m @ 100 mm @ f/2.8 version.

Brighter star on top left, is Rigel (β Orionis, mag 0.1). Brighter star down to the right of the comet is Sceptrum (53 Eridani, mag 3.9).

Darks (200), flats (50), and bias (173)

North is up.
