Comet 2014/Q2 Lovejoy @ 100 mm | 2015/january/10

Shot with an Canon EF 100mm f/2.8L Macro IS USM and a Canon EOS 1100D (unmodified). 4 h 4 m under suburban skys (strong sodium lighting hue), in 244 frames 1 minut each at 400 ISO/ASA. Sidereal tracking without guiding.

Darks, flats and bias

North is up. Brighthest start above the center of the image is ν Tauri (38 Tauri, mag 3.9), and the bright start on the center is 40 Tauri (mag 5.3), field of view is just less than 9º wide, and just less than 5º high ()
