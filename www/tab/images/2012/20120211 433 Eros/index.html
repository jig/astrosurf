433 Eros | 2012/feb/11

82 minutes long exposure of 433 Eros.

Exposures taken with SC8, LPR, f/6.3 Focal Reducer, Canon EOS 50D @ 400 ASA, autoguided, flats, darks and bias.

41 exposures 115 secongs long in 2 minutes interval, totalling 78 min 35 s.

North is up, the bright star on the left is HIP 50671 (mag 7.15, source Stellarium 0.11.0) and the bright star on top is HIP 50521 (mag 8.65). Field is 1º x 40'. Eros is travelling south on the picture. The dimming on the after the first third of the travel, might be due to asteroid rotation, but I'm not sure. The total travel of Eros in the picture, is about 3 arc minutes.
