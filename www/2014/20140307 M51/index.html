M51 Whirpool Galaxy | 2014/february-march

Shot with a Celestron Ultima 8 at prime focus (2030 mm), Canon EOS 1100D, LPR filter. 14 frames of 32 minutes m, 400 ISO/ASA, totalling 7h28m.

Shot through 3 days: 2014/february/22, 2014/february/28 and 2014/march/7. Frames on february 28th under sceptionally clear and steady sky, other two days average urban/suburban skies on Pallejà, Baix Llobregat (Catalonia).

Quality improvement respect M51 2012 shot: ASA 400, much longer frames, much more total exposition time, better polar alignment, much better scope collimation, and focusing was nailed with a Bahtinov mask.

darks, flats, and bias

North is up.

Tracking with PhD2 with a Starlight Lodestar in OAG, stacking with DSS, processing with CS5 (levels, curves, downsize and JPG conversion). Polar alignment was set with the help of the ad-hoc funcionality that has released on the new PHD2.
