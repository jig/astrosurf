Mars | 2012/april/8

Shot with an unmodified Philips ToUCam 740K on a Celestron 8 Ultima plus 17 mm eyepiece projection.

Seeing was not as bad as usual. 6000 frames (in two sequences) stacked with Registax6.

Syrtis Major is (clearly) visible but the north polar cap is varely visible (south up this time).
