M51 Whirlpool Galaxy | 2012/march

230 minutes total exposure time

Two series of exposures taken on march 30th and 31st with SC8, LPR, f/6.3 Focal Reducer, Canon EOS 50D, autoguided, flats, darks and bias.

First series (2012/mar/30) were 22 5 min exposures @1600 ASA

Second series (2012/mar/31) were 4 30 min exposures @400 ASA

Both series were stacked independently with DSS. Long exposure series were further processed by wavelets with Registax 6. Both results were summed with Photoshop CS5

Leftmost object is the farther 15,4 magnitude galaxy IC 4278. This is my (currently) limiting magnitude from my suburban skyies.
