M27 Dumbbell Nebula | 2012/june/22

Shot with a Celestron Ultima 8 at prime focus, Canon EOS 1100D and LPR filter. 7 frames, 5 minutes each, at 6400 ISO. Last two frames cleary "bluished" by dawn.

6 darks, no flats, no bias. (I still can afford not using flats, since the camera is new (no doghtnuts) and the object is compact)

North is up.

Tracking with MetaGuide on a 200 mm Nikkor Zoom and a Philips ToUCam 740k and MachGTO mount, stacking with DSS, processing with CS5 (levels, curves, downsize and JPG conversion)
