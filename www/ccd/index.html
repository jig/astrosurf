Photon capture devices

I use a Canon 50D for deep sky, and a Philips 740K ToUCam for planetary. I use a 1,25 inches adapter for the webcam, and a T-thread adapter for the Canon.

I use a Canon ACK-E2 power supply to feed the 50D.

I use the ToUCam for autoguiding as well, but sometimes it is difficult to find bright enought guiding stars (with the Nikkor at 200mm, I belive that I can guide on naked eye stars only)
